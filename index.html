How to play

  • Click the button to go to next level
  • Find it if you can
  • Catch it if you can
  • Try not to rage quit :)
  • Good luck!
  • If you think you can cheat... good luck with *that*
    (BTW you can if you know JS)

Goal of the game

  • Your goal is to complete all levels by finding and clicking all buttons
  • Some levels are easy, others are not
  • Some give you button in front, some hide it
  • Some buttons have siblings, other have legs (to run from you)
  • Let's see how fast are you in this
  • BTW order of levels is always same

Hints and information about the levels

  • Level 1: Try to catch.
  • Level 2: Find the correct one.
  • Level 3: Find and catch.
  • Level 4: To be honest, I add nothing in it, find in the possible areas.
  • Level 5: Click click and click.
  • Level 6: Find, find, find an easter egg (Hint "header").
  • Level 7: Click click, until your life fades away.
  • Level 8: Umm... I wonder what's under there.
  • Level 9: Good luck :)
  • Level 10: Get ready for chaos, turbo mode on.
  • Every tenth level is a boss level, with most chaos, may not be hard, but chaotic
